Agridera Fresh Market Banana Hybrids

Agridera aims to become one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of banana seeds. We invest
intensively in research and development to provide solutions for growers and consumers in numerous countries worldwide.
In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant cultivars adapted to different markets.

Currently, the main focus is on combining resistance to Banana Bunchy Top Virus (BBTV) and Panama Disease (TR4)
with other soil-borne diseases in high-quality banana breeding lines and hybrids. Moreover, comprehensive research is being conducted to
develop varieties resistant to new strains of fungal and viral diseases.

In terms of horticultural traits, breeding efforts are aimed at early maturity, different fruit sizes, firmness, bright yellow color, flavor, crack
resistance, extended shelf life, and adaptation to high and low temperatures. Combinations of quality features and disease resistance are sought
in various types of bananas, including dessert and cooking varieties.

Agridera's banana breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: Greenhouse Bananas,
Open-field Bananas, and Dwarf Bush-type Bananas.

Greenhouse Banana Breeding Program
This breeding program is aimed at the Spanish and Turkish markets. It focuses on both single and cluster harvesting, with an emphasis on round fruit shape and resistance to BBTV (Banana Bunchy Top Virus), TR4 (Tropical Race 4 – Panama Disease), nematodes, and Fusarium wilt. Some hybrids may include additional resistances, such as Black Sigatoka and bacterial wilt.
Open-field
Bananas
This breeding program is designed for the Brazilian, Moroccan, and Turkish markets. The main focus is on large-sized bananas (200 grams or more) with resistance to BBTV (Banana Bunchy Top Virus), TR4 (Tropical Race 4 – Panama Disease), nematodes, and Fusarium wilt. In the near future, resistance to Foc R3 (Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. cubense Race 3) will be included as a standard trait in our cultivars.
Open-field Bush-type
Bananas
This breeding program is designed for Mediterranean markets. We develop both round and elongated banana varieties, with standard sizes (140–180 grams) and larger fruit sizes (minimum 180 grams, reaching up to 300 grams or more). We incorporate resistance to BBTV (Banana Bunchy Top Virus), TR4 (Tropical Race 4 – Panama Disease), and nematodes as basic resistances in our hybrids. However, some hybrids may also include additional resistances, such as Black Sigatoka and bacterial wilt.

Agridera operates through international and local distributers around the world.
